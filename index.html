Machine Learning-Based Force Constant Extraction from INS Measurements of Polycrystalline Materials

We introduce a machine-learning based framework to uncover obscured phonon dynamics from two-dimensional spectra. By leveraging the power of variational autoencoder, we can obtain disentangled latent representation of spectra and successfully extract force constants needed to reconstruct phonon dispersions from this latent space. Importantly, our models trained on physics-based simulations can be effectively applied to experimental data as well, highlighting its versatility and generalizability.
