Yaokun Su is currently a fifth-year MSE Ph.D. candidate in Prof. Chen Li’s group at University of California, Riverside. He obtained his B.S. degree in Applied Physics from University of Science and Technology of China in 2018. His research focuses on understanding phonon, magnon and their interactions in materials by neutron scattering, atomistic simulation and machine learning.

My interdisciplinary research experience, which combines my expertise in machine learning with my background in materials science and physics, has prepared me well for the new challenges. As a highly motivated and enthusiastic learner, I am confident that I can quickly grasp new concepts and techniques necessary to contribute to the success of future projects. I also have experience working collaboratively with researchers from diverse backgrounds and translating complex technical concepts into actionable insights. I am excited to bring this unique perspective to a new stage and contribute to the development of cutting-edge technologies.
